Watch Dogs 2 is an open-world action-adventure game set in a fictional version of San Francisco . The game features a large open-world environment that players can explore, completing main and side missions, as well as various activities and challenges. Players can also use a variety of weapons and vehicles, and can engage in parkour-style movement to navigate the game's environment.
